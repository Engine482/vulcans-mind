# Vulcan’s Mind — Product Brief

## Definition

**Vulcan’s Mind** is a lightweight source-aware AI chatbot with own RAG, embedded into `motornyi.com`, answering about AI automation, RAG, mental health during war, psychometrics, military medicine, neuromodulation, cognitive performance, and human-AI interaction in the public context of Volodymyr “Vulcan” Motornyi’s work and interests.

## Purpose

The project demonstrates practical ability with AI API, RAG, chatbots, prompt engineering, backend/API integration, deployment, safety boundaries, and system thinking. It is intended as an interactive project artifact for employer review, not as a personal sales bot.

## Positioning

Vulcan’s Mind should sell ideas, systems thinking, and technical/product judgment — not directly “sell Volodymyr.” It should feel like an interface to a controlled set of public themes and sources.

## Users

Primary: potential employer, technical/operations lead, recruiter, or partner visiting `motornyi.com`.

Secondary: people interested in “Люстерко”, “Іменуй мене”, AI automation, digital mental health, military medicine constraints, and human-AI systems.

## Main themes

1. AI automation.
2. RAG, LLMs, chatbots, AI assistants.
3. Psychometrics and digital screening.
4. Wartime mental health.
5. Military medicine and field constraints.
6. Neuromodulation and device-based interventions.
7. Cognitive performance, learning, neurofitness.
8. Human-AI interaction and personal AI systems.

## Not a goal

Not a medical service, diagnostic system, psychotherapy tool, military decision-support system, universal ChatGPT clone, long-memory assistant, sales bot, or system for collecting personal data.

## Success criteria

A visitor can open the widget, ask 2–3 in-scope questions, receive useful source-aware answers, see sources, inspect GitHub, and understand the system architecture and boundaries within several minutes.
